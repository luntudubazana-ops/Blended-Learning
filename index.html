Blended Teaching, Learning and Assessment (BTLA) Guide

The BTLA Guide is your primary resource for navigating digital pedagogy, blended learning design, assessment, and AI at SU. Sections A–D cover foundational concepts through to practical strategies. Resources on this page support and extend the Guide — they do not replace it.

A: Digital pedagogy foundations B: SUNLearn course design C: Digitally enabled assessment D: Teaching with AI

BTLA Guide · CLT · SU
Design principle: Resources within each pathway that already appear in the BTLA Guide are clearly marked ↗ See Guide (Section X) — this signals to lecturers that the Guide is the canonical source, and they are directed back into it rather than away from it. No duplicate content is hosted here; instead the page acts as a wayfinding layer over the Guide.
